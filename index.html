my favorite places

DOMINICAN REPUBLIC

i visted this tropical paradise in 2019 and 2021

Dominican Republic has many areas that you can visit but I highly recommend that you stay at a resort in Punta Cana. It has many attractions to see such as beautiful beaches with clear water that you could go snorkeling or jetskiing in. They also have extremely nice restraunts where you could try their local cuisine.







movie reviews

Encanto
Magical Musical

This was an amazing movie! Everything was on point from its characters to the emotional rollercoaster ride that it took us on. I loved every single character and all the music was spectacular and extremeley catchy. It had a great story that could make you tear up so keep your tissues handy! It was made by Lin Maunel Miranda who is known for making amazing musicals and movies. The creators of good dinosaur and coco were also a part of the crew. I highly recommend this movie!







tv reviews

KC Undercover
Enjoyable Tv Series

This was such a good tv show to watch! You always enjoyed every episode and there all the actors did amazing and made it so you can't really imagine anyone else playing their characters. It was one of my favorite disney tv series and still is to this day! Although i'm sad it ended I hope in the future they start a reboot or reunion between all the actors or show where they ended up. If your looking for a show that is funny but there is still action and fighting scenes this show might be a good one to watch!
